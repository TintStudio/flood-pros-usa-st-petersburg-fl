Mold Testing in South Pasadena, FL

Air Quality Testing

Air quality testing is used to detect airborne mold spores, which can negatively affect the health of those inside the property. Our team collects air samples from various locations in your South Pasadena, FL property, which are then analyzed in a certified laboratory to determine the concentration of mold spores in the air.

If the air quality test reveals high levels of mold spores, it indicates the presence of mold in your property, even if it’s not visible. This information helps us decide the best course of action for mold removal and improving indoor air quality.

Surface Sample Testing

Surface sample testing involves taking samples from surfaces suspected to have mold, such as walls, floors, or furniture. These samples are analyzed in a certified lab to determine the type and concentration of mold present on the surfaces of your property.

This method is ideal for identifying mold growth that may not be visible but is present on surfaces throughout your South Pasadena, FL property. The results help us understand the scope of the problem and tailor the mold removal process to effectively address the contamination.

ERMI or HERTSMI Testing

ERMI (Environmental Relative Moldiness Index) and HERTSMI (Home Environmental Relative Moldiness Index) testing are advanced methods used to evaluate the level of mold contamination in a property. These tests assess the genetic markers of mold species, helping to identify hidden mold that might not be visible but could still affect the health of the occupants.

Flood Pros USA – St. Petersburg, FL uses ERMI and HERTSMI testing to provide a more detailed understanding of the mold situation in your South Pasadena, FL property. These tests are especially beneficial for individuals with allergies or respiratory problems as they help identify harmful mold species.
