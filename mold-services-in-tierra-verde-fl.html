Surface Sample Testing

Surface sample testing involves collecting physical samples from surfaces where mold may be growing, such as walls, floors, and furniture. These samples are analyzed in a certified lab to identify the type and level of mold present.

Surface testing allows us to confirm whether mold is present and helps us determine the best approach for removal. This testing is especially useful for identifying mold that may not be visible but is present on various surfaces within your property.

ERMI or HERTSMI Testing

ERMI (Environmental Relative Moldiness Index) and HERTSMI (Home Environmental Relative Moldiness Index) testing are advanced methods used to assess mold exposure based on the specific genetic markers of mold species. These tests provide a deeper understanding of mold contamination and can detect mold that may not be immediately visible in your Tierra Verde, FL property.

Flood Pros USA – St. Petersburg, FL utilizes ERMI and HERTSMI testing when needed to identify potentially harmful mold strains. These tests are especially beneficial for people with respiratory issues or allergies, as they help identify mold types that could exacerbate health problems.
