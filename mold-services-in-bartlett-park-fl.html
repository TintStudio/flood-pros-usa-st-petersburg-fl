Mold Testing in Bartlett Park, FL

Mold testing is essential for identifying the type and concentration of mold present in your property. At Flood Pros USA – St. Petersburg, FL, we offer comprehensive mold testing services in Bartlett Park, FL to help you understand the extent of your mold issue. We utilize advanced testing methods such as air quality testing, surface sample collection, and specific tests like ERMI or HERTSMI to deliver accurate results.

Air Quality Testing

Air quality testing is an important step in detecting airborne mold spores that can affect indoor air quality. Our specialists use high-precision equipment to collect air samples from various locations within your property. This data helps us determine the concentration of mold spores in the air, which can directly impact the health of anyone living or working in the building.

We compare the results with the standards set by regulatory bodies to assess if mold levels are harmful. If necessary, we recommend remediation measures to reduce mold exposure and improve air quality.

Surface Sample Testing

Surface sample testing involves taking physical samples from surfaces suspected of having mold growth. This could include walls, floors, or other materials in your Bartlett Park, FL property. Our team sends these samples to certified laboratories for analysis, where the type of mold and its concentration are identified.

Surface testing helps us confirm the presence of mold in areas that may not be visible to the naked eye. This allows us to take targeted actions for mold removal and remediation.

ERMI or HERTSMI Testing

ERMI (Environmental Relative Moldiness Index) and HERTSMI (Home Environmental Relative Moldiness Index) testing are advanced methods to assess mold exposure based on specific genetic markers. These tests are particularly useful for determining the presence of hidden mold in your property.

At Flood Pros USA – St. Petersburg, FL, we use ERMI or HERTSMI testing when necessary to get a deeper understanding of the mold contamination in your Bartlett Park, FL property. These tests can be especially helpful for residents with respiratory issues or allergies, providing a clear picture of potential health risks.

Post-Remediation Testing
