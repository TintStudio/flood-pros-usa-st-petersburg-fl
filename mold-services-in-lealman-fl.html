Mold Testing in Lealman, FL

Mold testing is a crucial step in understanding the extent of mold contamination in your Lealman, FL property. At Flood Pros USA – St. Petersburg, FL, we offer detailed mold testing services to help identify the type, concentration, and source of the mold. Using advanced methods, we provide you with accurate data to determine the next steps in mold removal and remediation.

Air Quality Testing

Air quality testing helps detect airborne mold spores that can negatively affect the health of your family or employees. Our team collects air samples from various locations within your Lealman, FL property using specialized equipment. These samples are sent to a certified lab for analysis to determine the concentration of mold spores in the air.

Flood Pros USA – St. Petersburg, FL uses the results to assess if mold levels are high enough to cause health problems and recommend any necessary steps for remediation to improve indoor air quality.

Surface Sample Testing

Surface sample testing involves taking samples from surfaces suspected of mold growth, such as walls, floors, and ceilings. These samples are sent to a laboratory where they are analyzed to determine the type and concentration of mold present. This method is useful for identifying hidden mold that may not be visible to the naked eye.

Surface testing helps us confirm the presence of mold and assess the extent of contamination on specific surfaces within your Lealman, FL property, allowing us to tailor the mold removal approach accordingly.

ERMI or HERTSMI Testing

ERMI (Environmental Relative Moldiness Index) and HERTSMI (Home Environmental Relative Moldiness Index) testing are advanced methods used to assess mold exposure based on the genetic markers of mold species. These tests are especially useful for detecting hidden mold and understanding the overall mold contamination in your Lealman, FL property.

At Flood Pros USA – St. Petersburg, FL, we use ERMI and HERTSMI testing when necessary to provide a more comprehensive understanding of the mold contamination in your property. These tests are particularly beneficial for individuals with respiratory issues or allergies as they help identify mold types that may cause health problems.

Post-Remediation Testing

Post-remediation testing is performed after mold removal and remediation to verify that the mold has been successfully removed and that your property is safe. Flood Pros USA – St. Petersburg, FL conducts thorough post-remediation testing to ensure that mold levels are within safe limits and that no mold spores are present in your Lealman, FL property.
