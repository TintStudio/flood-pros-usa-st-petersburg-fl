Air Quality Testing

Air quality testing helps detect airborne mold spores that could affect the health of the occupants. Using specialized equipment, we collect air samples from various locations within your property to analyze the concentration of mold spores in the air. High levels of airborne spores can cause respiratory issues, allergies, and other health problems.

Flood Pros USA – St. Petersburg, FL conducts thorough air quality testing, providing you with valuable data to assess whether mold is affecting the air you breathe. If necessary, we will recommend remediation steps to improve the air quality in your Kenneth City, FL property.

Surface Sample Testing

Surface sample testing involves taking samples from surfaces suspected of having mold growth, such as walls, floors, and furniture. These samples are analyzed in a certified lab to determine the type and concentration of mold present on your property.

Surface testing is particularly useful for identifying mold that may not be visible but is present on surfaces throughout your home or business. The results help us tailor our mold removal approach to ensure that mold is completely eliminated from your Kenneth City, FL property.

ERMI or HERTSMI Testing

ERMI (Environmental Relative Moldiness Index) and HERTSMI (Home Environmental Relative Moldiness Index) testing are advanced techniques used to assess mold exposure based on genetic markers of specific mold species. These tests provide a more comprehensive understanding of mold contamination in your Kenneth City, FL property, even in areas that may not show visible mold growth.

At Flood Pros USA – St. Petersburg, FL, we use ERMI and HERTSMI testing to evaluate the level of mold contamination and identify harmful mold types that may not be detected through traditional methods. These tests are especially beneficial for individuals with respiratory issues or allergies.
