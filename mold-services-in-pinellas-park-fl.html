Mold Testing in Pinellas Park, FL

Mold testing is an essential service to accurately assess the type and concentration of mold present in your Pinellas Park, FL property. Our testing services help guide the mold remediation process and ensure the best solutions for your home or business.

Air Quality Testing

Air quality testing is an important tool for detecting airborne mold spores, which can affect the health of the building's occupants. We collect air samples from different areas of your Pinellas Park, FL property and send them to a certified laboratory for analysis. The results give us a clear indication of the concentration of mold spores in the air, helping us assess the level of contamination.

If high levels of mold spores are found in the air, we will recommend specific steps for remediation to improve air quality and eliminate the mold problem.

Surface Sample Testing

Surface sample testing involves collecting samples from surfaces where mold is suspected to be growing, such as walls, floors, or furniture. These samples are sent to a lab for analysis to determine the type and concentration of mold present. This testing helps us identify hidden mold and understand the scope of the contamination.

Surface testing is especially valuable in determining the most effective mold removal approach, allowing us to focus on areas that are most affected by mold growth.

ERMI or HERTSMI Testing

ERMI (Environmental Relative Moldiness Index) and HERTSMI (Home Environmental Relative Moldiness Index) testing are advanced methods that assess mold contamination based on genetic markers of mold species. These tests are used to detect hidden mold and provide a comprehensive view of mold exposure in your Pinellas Park, FL property.

Flood Pros USA – St. Petersburg, FL uses ERMI and HERTSMI testing when necessary to identify mold strains that may not be visible but could still pose health risks. These tests are especially helpful for people with respiratory issues or allergies, as they help identify the specific types of mold that might affect health.
