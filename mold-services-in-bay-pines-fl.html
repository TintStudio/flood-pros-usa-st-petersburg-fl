Mold Testing in Bay Pines, FL

Mold testing is essential to determine the type and concentration of mold in your Bay Pines, FL property. Air Quality Testing

Air quality testing is an effective method to detect airborne mold spores that can negatively impact your health. Our team uses specialized equipment to collect air samples from various areas of your Bay Pines, FL property. These samples are analyzed in a certified laboratory to determine the concentration of mold spores in the air.

If the air quality test reveals high levels of mold spores, it indicates that mold is present, and remediation will be necessary. Our air quality testing helps ensure that your indoor environment is safe to occupy and that your health is not at risk from mold exposure.

Surface Sample Testing

Surface sample testing involves taking samples from visible or suspected mold-affected surfaces, such as walls, ceilings, and floors. These samples are sent to a laboratory for analysis to determine the type and level of mold present. Surface testing is useful for identifying mold that may not be immediately visible but is present on surfaces throughout your property.

This testing helps us pinpoint the areas where mold is most concentrated and decide on the best course of action for removal and remediation. It provides valuable insight into the scope of the problem, allowing us to tailor our approach accordingly.

ERMI or HERTSMI Testing

ERMI (Environmental Relative Moldiness Index) and HERTSMI (Home Environmental Relative Moldiness Index) testing are advanced methods for assessing mold contamination based on the genetic markers of specific mold species. These tests help identify hidden mold that may not be visible to the naked eye and provide a deeper understanding of the mold contamination in your Bay Pines, FL property.

Flood Pros USA – St. Petersburg, FL uses ERMI and HERTSMI testing when necessary to provide a comprehensive view of the mold situation in your property. These tests are particularly helpful for people with respiratory issues or allergies, as they can identify harmful mold strains that might be causing health problems.
